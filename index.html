Objective

The IOSC-BVP club was inaugurated on Nov 7,2022 to bridge the existing skill between college graduates and industry expectations. To address this problem, the club will work on industry-academic linkages and offer trainings, workshops and projects in emerging technologies.

Vision & Mission

The Vision of IOSC-BVP club is to help the college students to keep pace with fast changing industry needs and expectations. The club will equip the students with industry relevant skills and build a strong reputation in the industry. This in turn will make the students more employable in the future
